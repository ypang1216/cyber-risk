About Cyber Risk Segmentation

Our Mission

At CyberRisk Insights, our mission is to democratize access to sophisticated cyber risk assessment and management tools for startups and small-to-medium sized businesses. We believe that every organization, regardless of size, deserves to understand their cyber risk exposure and have access to appropriate insurance coverage.

By leveraging data analytics and machine learning algorithms, we provide startups with actionable insights into their cyber risk profile, helping them make informed decisions about security investments and insurance coverage.

Our Methodology

Risk Segmentation Framework

Our risk segmentation framework is built on analyzing five key risk categories that are particularly relevant to startups:

  • Data Breach Risk: Evaluation of your data security practices, the sensitivity of data you handle, and your vulnerability to breaches.
  • Ransomware Exposure: Assessment of your susceptibility to ransomware attacks based on your technology infrastructure and backup practices.
  • Third-party Vendor Risk: Analysis of the security posture of your critical vendors and service providers.
  • Employee Security Risk: Evaluation of security awareness among your team and vulnerability to social engineering attacks.
  • Cloud Security Risk: Assessment of your cloud infrastructure security configuration and compliance.

Data Sources

Our risk assessments are powered by multiple data sources:

  • Industry benchmarking data from over 10,000 companies
  • Historical cyber incident data from various sectors
  • Security questionnaire responses
  • External vulnerability scan results (optional)
  • Company size, industry, and technology profile information

Insurance Partners

We work with leading cyber insurance providers to help startups find appropriate coverage based on their specific risk profile. Our platform helps match your organization with insurers that specialize in your industry and risk category.
